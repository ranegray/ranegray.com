# ranegray.com

This is my personal website.

It is a small home base for my robotics work, research, writing, and selected projects. Most of what I care about right now sits somewhere around perception, manipulation, autonomy, embodied AI, and low-cost robotics hardware.

The site is intentionally simple. I mostly want it to be a clean place to point people when they ask what I am working on, what I have built, or what direction my research is moving.
